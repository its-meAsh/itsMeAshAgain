How to play?
  • Every block can have 2 outcomes, safezone or a mine.
  • Your objective is to find all the safezones without opening any mine. The number on the safezone depicts the number of mines adjactent to the safezone, up, down, diagonal, left and right.
  • Once you find all the safezones, the game ends.
Controls
  • LMB (Left Mouse Button) to open
  • MMB (Middle Mouse Button) to add or remove flag
  • Mouse required for flagging facility
