Color Matcher
This project is a game, where you have to control 3 channels - R,G,B to match the color shown in the left square. Your channel values are shown in the right square with it's Hexadecimal code written. Then click on the check button to know your accuracy and the actual color. In the top right corner lies the Extended version button, on clicking it, the game extends to 4 channels - R,G,B,A where A controls the opacity of the color, and now the difficulty increases. Every channel has an H button at their right, it is for hint which you can take for only 1 channel. The accuracy is calculated on the basis of the sum of similarity in the actual hex code and your answer.

Rock Paper Scissor
It is a game which I bet there's no one who doesn't know about it. Rock Paper Scissors an alternative way of coin tossing to get a result. It is played between 2 or many players where each have to choose one move - Rock, Paper or Scissors. The decision is made depending upon the moves and it continues. Here you can play it with an AI opponent not desgined strictly so their are good chances that you win.
Reaction
It is a test-type game where you have to click on the blue sized ball whose size reduces every click. It randomly moves on the screen after every click and your job is to clock as fast as possible on it.
Tic Tac Toe
World famous game played by every human also known as Cross-Zero or X-O. You have a 3 by 3 grid, place your moves to make a combination of your token in any direction. Here you can play it with your friends in 1v1 mode and if you are lonely, then play it with the AI desgined by me. It's algorithm is loosely desgined and does not perform the tricky moves which stucks the opponent. Thinking to add it soon...
Connect4
A famous game played between 2 players. Their aim is put 4 of their tokens either horizontally, vertically or diagonally. You need to click on the column number present below every column to place your move. It can be played between 2 humans or a human and an AI designed by me. Its algorithm is pretty loose so don't celebrate too much if you win.
